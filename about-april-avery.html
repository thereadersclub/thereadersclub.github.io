APRIL AVERY

April Avery is a passionate writer who loves exploring the realms of fantasy and romance. She has been sharing her stories on Wattpad for over five years, captivating readers with her imaginative plots and relatable characters. With a strong focus on emotional depth and intricate world-building, April's stories transport readers into captivating worlds where love and magic intertwine. When she's not writing, April enjoys sipping tea, cuddling up with a good book, and spending time with her loyal cat, Luna. April is a twenty something dreamer, a Lupus and ITP fighter, and a storyteller who enjoys rainy afternoons, chasing sunsets, and ocean breeze. Be her guest to her unending thoughts and the universe inside her head.
